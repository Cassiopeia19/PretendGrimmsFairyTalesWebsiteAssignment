Welcome!

On this website, you'll find four stories from the Brothers Grimm. The four stories available on this website are:

  • The Traveling Musicians
  • The Frog Prince
  • Rapunzel
  • Hansel & Gretel

These stories, along with all of the other fairy tales written by the Brothers Grimm, are freely available through Project Guttenberg , the world's largest collection of free electronic books. The four stories have delighted generations of children and we hope you enjoy them as well.
